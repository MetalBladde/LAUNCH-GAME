About Launch

Launch is a passion project developed by a small team of space enthusiasts and game developers. Our goal was to create a game that captures the wonder and excitement of rhythm based pokemon games while providing engaging gameplay mechanics.

The game has been in development for over a month, We've combined mechanics of FNF and some of pokemon to create our dreams

Gameplay

In Launch, players fight other enemies to assert dominance over the battle field

  • Moveset timed presses
  • Pick strategic moves
  • Enjoy the game
