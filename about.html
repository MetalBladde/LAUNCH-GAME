About Launch

Launch is a passion project developed by a small team of space enthusiasts and game developers. Our goal was to create a game that captures the wonder and excitement of space exploration while providing engaging gameplay mechanics.

The game has been in development for over two years, with a focus on creating a realistic yet accessible space simulation. We've combined elements of physics-based flight with creative freedom to allow players to experience space travel in a way that's both challenging and rewarding.

Gameplay

In Launch, players take on the role of a space explorer tasked with discovering new worlds and unraveling the mysteries of the universe. The game features:

  • Realistic orbital mechanics and physics
  • A dynamic economy and trading system
  • Alien civilizations and diplomacy
  • Resource gathering and crafting
  • Epic space battles

Whether you prefer peaceful exploration, intense combat, or economic domination, Launch offers multiple paths to success in its vast universe.
